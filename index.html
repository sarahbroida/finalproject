About Me

Welcome to Greendale Community College, you’re already accepted! Unfortunately, there’s no actual Greendale, but I do have the next best thing—a site dedicated to celebrating the quirky, award-winning show Community! I am a huge fan of the series who decided to channel my love of the show into an online space where other fans can join together. On this site, you'll find episode breakdowns, character profiles, fan-submitted theories, and a vast collection of trivia and quizzes.

Also make sure to sign up for our newsletter and receive a free pdf of the fan-created (yes, me again) Community Monopoly Board!
