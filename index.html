About Us

Welcome to Greendale Community College! Well, not the actual school, but the next best thing—a fan page dedicated to celebrating the award-winning TV show Community! We are a team of die-hard fans just like you who decided to channel our love for the adventures of Jeff, Britta, Abed, Annie, Troy, Shirley, and Pierce into creating a place where other fans can gather. You'll find detailed episode breakdowns, character deep-dives, fan theories, and a collection of trivia and quizzes. Let’s make some magic with our keyboards and keep the Greendale spirit alive! And remember, in the words of Abed, "Cool. Cool, cool, cool."
